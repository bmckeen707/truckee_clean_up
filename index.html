Truckee Clean Up

Truckee is the home of many beautiful ecosystems that are constantly at risk due to a large tourist industry and a growing human population. One way we can protect our natural resources is to clean up trash anytime you see it! You might be the last person to see that wrapper on the ground before it becomes burried and degraded into microplastics.

A designated dry bag can be clipped to the outside of a backpack and rinsed out afterwards!

If you see doggy bags grab one and fill it with trash!

If you see trash pick it up!
